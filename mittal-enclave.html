MITTAL ENCLAVE     | NAIGAON

Mittal Enclave was conceived with a simple, yet a laudable idea in mind. Most of the new apartment complexes across Mumbai don't align with the budgets of a common Mumbaikar. Mittal Enclave aims to provide a world-class living experience for the ones who want to avoid the extremely high prices, for a similar lifestyle in Mumbai. Built over more than 50 acres of land, this 45 building project is constructed using the latest construction techniques and offers the best of amenities. From prayer areas to banking facilities, recreation zones to shopping marts - you get them all at Mittal Enclave. With a plan to develop an IT park within the township, we also aim to offer job opportunities for the residents, in their own backyard.
